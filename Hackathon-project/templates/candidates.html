Meet the Candidates

Welcome to the candidates page for Vote.org Here, you can learn about the individuals vying for president in the upcoming election. We believe in providing voters with comprehensive information to make informed decisions, so let's introduce you to the candidates:

Cyril Ramaphosa

Political Party Affiliation: African National Congress (ANC)

International Relations: Ramaphosa has been active in international diplomacy, representing South Africa on the global stage and advocating for issues such as climate change, peace, and economic development.

John Steenhuisen

Political Party Affiliation: Democratic Alliance (DA)

International Relations: Steenhuisen has engaged in international relations, representing the DA's stance on various global issues and advocating for South Africa's interests on the international stage.

Julius Malema

Political Party Affiliation: The leader and founder of the Economic Freedom Fighters (EFF),

International Relations: Malema has expressed support for various international causes, including solidarity with other leftist movements across Africa and the world.
